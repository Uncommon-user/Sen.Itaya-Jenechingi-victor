Sen. Itaya Jenechingi Victor

Obedient Leader | Student Advocate | Visionary

Biography

Sen. Itaya Jenechingi Victor is a native of Akamkpa Urban Community in Akamkpa Local Government Area of Cross River State, Nigeria. Born into the Royal family of Ntufam Felix Nyong Itaya.

Education

He began his education at Government Primary School Akamkpa and Njagachang Community Primary School (FSLC 2012). He attended Government Day Secondary School Akamkpa and Government Secondary School Akamkpa (GOSSA), where he obtained SSCE in 2018.

He holds an NCE in Political Science/Social Studies from Cross River State College of Education, Awi (2021), and is currently studying B.Sc. Ed Social Studies at Cross River State College of Education Akamkpa (in affiliation with University of Calabar).

Leadership & Service

Sen. Itaya is a humanitarian and student-friendly leader whose passion for students’ welfare is unmatched. He has held several positions within and outside the institution, promoting effective student unionism and ensuring that student voices are heard.

Vision & Mission

His vision is to positively transform student welfarism, strengthen student-management relationships, empower students through strategic partnerships, and improve social activities at the University of Education and Entrepreneurship, Akamkpa.
